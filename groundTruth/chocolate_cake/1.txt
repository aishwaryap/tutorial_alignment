1|Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
2|Grease and flour three 6in x 1.5in round cake pans.
3|Mix together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and baking soda. 
3|Set aside.
3|In a large bowl, beat butter, eggs and vanilla.
3|Gradually add sugar.
3|Beat on medium to high speed for about 3-4 minutes until well mixed
3|Alternately combine in flour mixture and milk to batter while beating.
3|Continue to beat until batter is smooth.
4|Pour equal amounts of batter into greased and floured round cake pans.
4|Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
4|Check with a toothpick to se if it is done. Bake a few minutes more, if needed.
5|Remove from oven and allow cakes to cool in pans for a few minutes.
5|Place cakes on a wire rack, to them allow to completely cool.
