1|Along with the groceries, you will need a butter knife for spreading, and a plate.
1|Take two pieces of bread from the bag and lay them on the plate.
2|Open peanut butter. 
2|Using the knife, spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread until the bread has a coating layer on it. 
2|Replace top on jar. Wipe off knife.
3|Open the jelly. 
3|Spread jelly on the other slice of bread, once again giving it a generous coating. 
3|Replace top on jar.
4|Take the slice of bread with jelly and lay it on top of the peanut buttered piece, aligning the sides and corners. 
4|Take the knife and cut the sandwich, either widthwise or diagonally.
4|Put peanut butter and jelly back in refrigerator and bread away.
