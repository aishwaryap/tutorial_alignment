1|Open the bread and remove two slices, laying them side by side.
2|Open peanut butter jar.
2|Remove some peanut butter with the knife.
2|Spread the peanut butter onto the bread until you have as much as you want, removing more from the jar as needed.
3|Open jelly jar.
3|Remove a table spoonful of jelly.
3|Spread the jelly on the bread until you have as much as you want, removing more from the jar as needed.
4|Place the two slices together, peanut butter and jelly facing inwards, press down slightly, and enjoy
