1|Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
2|Butter cake pans. 
2|Line bottoms with parchment paper, and butter paper. 
2|Dust the pans with cocoa; tap out extra.
3|Sift cocoa, flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and sugar into bowl. 
3|Beat in oil, buttermilk, vanilla, eggs, and hot water one at a time, using a mixer set at low. 
3|Beat until smooth, about 2 minutes.
4|Pour batter into pans. 
4|Bake 45 to 55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the centers comes out clean; an adult should rotate the pans halfway through and remove cakes from the oven.
5|Let cakes cool in pans on wire racks, 20 minutes, before inverting to remove. 
5|Discard paper. 
5|Cool completely on racks, top side up. 
5|An adult should cut off top of one layer.
5|With a butter knife, frost top of trimmed layer.
5|Place the other layer on top. 
6|Frost top and sides of cake.
